### Why become a member?

Being a member helps support important work and gives you access to better tools.

- You can follow up on previous identifications.
- You receive alerts when answers are ready.
- You help improve the system.

### Membership Types

**Free:** Submit anonymously and view your responses.

**Registered:** Access history, follow-up, and priority service.
